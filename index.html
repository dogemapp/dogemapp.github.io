Welcome to DogEm!

SP-04 DogEm is a project for CS 4850 - Fall 2022 - Section 03

DogEm is a cross-platform (iOS, Android, and web) application that can be

used to persistently message your contacts via Email, Call, and Text

until you get a reply.
